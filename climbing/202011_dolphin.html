Dolphin - 5.7 - October 2020 - JTNP, CA

Commentary: Probably the best moderate off-width (is that phrase even legal?) in Joshua Tree. Easy climbing from the start to a short but will seem like forever off-width section you're either too big or too small for. I'll definitely be back for that clean send.

The basics

  • Last Ascent: November 23th, 2020
  • Party: 2
  • Rating: 5.7
  • Lead: 1 take

Gear

  • C4: doubles from 2-4, single 5, 6
  • Alpine draws: 10
  • Rope: 1x 70m
  • Other stuff: 2x lockers for anchor

Notes

  • Anchor is bolted to climbers left.
  • Rap off of anchors to the right of Bird of Fire, which are ... questionable
  • Dump doubles of 2-4 early and start bumping 5, then bump 6 to top
