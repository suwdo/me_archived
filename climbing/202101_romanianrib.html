Romanian Rib - January 2021 - Red Rock, NV

The basics

This is a great first route for starting at First Creek. The climb itself is low 5th class, but the descent will take some route finding to make sure you don't accidentally cliff yourself out and have to backtrack. 70m rope was used to rap.

  • Last Ascent: January 2nd, 2021
  • Party: 2
  • 8-10 hours car to car
  • Rating: 5.3
  • Lead: all pitches

Gear

  • Totem: Single from black to green
  • C4: Single from 1-2
  • Alpine draws: 10
  • Rope: 1x 70m
  • Other stuff: 2x double length slings
