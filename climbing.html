Cat in the Hat - 5.6 - October 2020 - Red Rock, NV

Commentary: Cat in the hat is a chill, but interesting enough multipitch. Great route to familiarize yourself with Red Rock trad multis.

The basics

  • Personal First Ascent: October 30th, 2020
  • Party: 3 (single rope with ascender)
  • Time: 8 hours car to car
  • Rating: 5.6
  • Lead: all pitches

Gear

  • Totem: double from blue to purple, single black and green
  • C4: double from 1 to 3, single 0.75, 4
  • Nuts: none
  • Alpine draws: 10
  • Rope: 1x 70m
  • Other stuff: 2x lockers, 2x double length slings

Commentary: If you're a somewhat competent climber (e.g. solid Joshua Tree 5.7 climber), you won't use half of this gear per pitch, but never hurts to have extras?

Frogland - October 2020 - Red Rock, NV

The basics

You do Frogland for the chockstone squeeze pitch. Just do it. You know you want to. Make some grunting noises, release your anger.

  • Personal First Ascent: October 3rd, 2020
  • Party: 2
  • 10 hours car to car
  • Rating: 5.8 PG-13
  • Lead: pitch 3-6
